About the Department

In this modern world to compete with the world countries we need to habituate ourselves with the use of technological equipment. So opening such a department (CSE) was a notable decision.

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering is therefore aware of providing quality education. The department provides an excellent and bright young brand of teachers who are deeply committed to the university to bring out the best graduates in academic disciplines and contribute to the development of the ICT backbone of the nation.

Our graduates are serving the nation with reputation. Currently, we are offering B.Sc. (Engg.) and MS degrees. However, we also dream of commencing a PhD program in the future.
